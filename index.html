I'm Jack, a machine learning PhD student at the University of Cambridge and an Alan Turing Institute Enrichment Scheme Student

Supervised by Dr.Alexandra Brintrup and a member of the SCAIL research group, My research focuses on robust machine learning, tackling problems such as unlearning, continual learning, and out of distribution data.

Prior to my PhD, I worked on projects in nuclear robotics and statistical simulations of human neuro-imaging data, as well as gaining experience as a software engineer in industry.
